About Us

Wealth Dividends Ltd is a private holding company focused on generating passive income through automated high-yield dividend capture strategies. Operating as a closed financial mechanism, we take sums of capital and return payouts on a regular monthly basis, providing consistent income to our shareholders. Currently, the company is in the experimental phase of refining its software for internal use only.

Payout Strategy

Wealth Dividends Ltd employs proprietary software to identify and capture dividends from high-yield stocks. While the software captures dividends multiple times a year (potentially 50-100 times), the company consolidates these returns and distributes them to shareholders on a monthly basis. This ensures steady, predictable payouts regardless of the timing of individual dividend payments.

Frequency and Typical Returns

  • Monthly dividend payouts are distributed to shareholders.
  • Typical returns: 6-8% annually from dividend income.
  • Potential additional income from property investments, with yields of 5-6% annually.
