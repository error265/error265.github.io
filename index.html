About Us

Wealth Dividends Ltd is a private holding company dedicated to generating passive income through innovative software that automates high-yield dividend capture strategies. Our company operates as a closed financial mechanism, paying dividends only to its shareholders. Currently, we are in the experimental phase of automating dividend strategies for internal use.

Payout Strategy

Wealth Dividends Ltd generates income through high-yield dividend stocks. Our proprietary software identifies the best opportunities to capture dividends. Dividends are paid out quarterly or semi-annually, depending on stock performance and market conditions.

Frequency and Typical Returns

  • Dividend payments are expected 4-6 times per year.
  • Typical returns: 6-8% annually from dividend income.
  • Potential additional income from property investments, yielding 5-6% annually.

Current Status

The company is currently in the experimental phase, focusing on refining its software to optimize dividend capture. Wealth Dividends Ltd does not recruit customers and does not market financial products or offer investment advice. All investments are private and managed solely by its Shareholders.
